<header class="major">
    <h3>Topics</h3>
</header>

<p>From a technical point of view, we will study videogame history, anatomy of game engines, Unreal Engine, Blueprint scripting, programming best practices, OpenGL and GPU basics, animation and rigging, game physics. As videogames are a product of connecting art and techinques, we will also study gameplay, storytelling and narrative. We will also introduce Unity and Godot game engines.</p>

<p>No textbook is required, but if you want to buy one, "Game Engine Architecture" by Jason Gregory is a very comprehensive book that covers every aspect for an initiation to game development.</p>
